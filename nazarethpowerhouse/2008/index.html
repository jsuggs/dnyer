Our vision in creating a full-service Health and Fitness Center sprang from our desire to procide a place for people of all ages and fitness levels to enhange their personal health and wellness in a warm, friendly environment. Although our health and fitness center is a franchise with Powerhouse International, it is owned and operated locally by family members who care deeply about their community.
We offer reasonably priced amenities such as Personal Training, Nutrition, Massage, and Child Care. Trannign services will soon be added to our roster as well. All of these components allow us to offer a comprehensive approach to personalized fitness packages for every member.
Since the opening of our Pre-Sales office in September 2007, we have actively sought your concerns and suggestions so that we can customize our facility to better serve our clients. As we move forward, we plan to develop an additional 11,000 square feet, ever mindful of the needs of our members.
A commitment to family and community is of paramount importance to us, and public response has been tremendous. Our classes are a huge success, and we currently offer 28 different classes each week in these areas:
  • Indoor Cycling (Beginner and Advanced)
  • Cardio Kickboxing
  • Total Body Strength
  • 20/20 & Total Body Strength
  • Pilates
  • Power Yoga
  • Yoga Fusion
  • Circuit Training
  • Boot Camp 101
As you well know, a healthy, well-adjusted employee is a valuable asset. Nazareth Powerhouse can offer your company programs that will alleviate stress and tension, resulting in healthier and more productive employees. And as a further incentive to you, we offer corporate membership discounts.
We will also offer a Youth Orientation Program geared toward 11 to 16-year-olds. This comprehensive approach will provide direction to participants in safety, exercise techniques, and gym etiquette. By Targeting young people, we can help to inspire them to adopt a healthy and fit lifestyle that can become a natural part of their lives.
It is an honor and privilege for us to serve our community, providing vital services for a healthier living with our first-class staff of professionals. We look forward to discussing our discount options with you and to welcome you personally to our gym.
