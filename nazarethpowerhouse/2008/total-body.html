Total Body Strength

Total Body Strength: Increase your muscular endurance and lean muscle by joining us for a great group weight-training program. This class will incorporate hand weights, free weights, body bars, resistance bands as well as ones own body weight to help members tone and shape their muscles. Class design will focus on the major muscle groups with proper form and technique and applying appropriate resistance levels to achieve your own individual goals. This class is for all levels of training.
